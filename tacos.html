Baked Chicken Tacos

What makes them stand out is the crispy taco shells that are baked to perfection in the oven, offering a satisfying crunch without the need for deep-frying. Baked Chicken Tacos are not only a crowd-pleaser but also a convenient and fuss-free way to enjoy the beloved flavors of tacos with a wholesome twist.

Prep time

15 min.

Cook time

15 min.

Serves

serves 5

Instructions

Step-1

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper. In a large oven-safe skillet or baking dish, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until they start to brown. Remove the chicken breasts from the skillet and set them aside.

Step-2

In the same skillet, add the chopped onion and minced garlic. Sauté for about 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent. Add the taco seasoning mix and stir it into the onion and garlic mixture. Pour in the chicken broth and stir to combine. Let it simmer for a few minutes to thicken slightly.

Step-3

Return the chicken breasts to the skillet and coat them with the sauce. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through (the internal temperature should reach 165°F or 74°C). Once cooked, remove the chicken from the oven, and using two forks, shred it into bite-sized pieces right in the skillet. Stir the shredded chicken into the sauce to coat evenly.

Step-4

Warm the taco shells or tortillas according to the package instructions. Lay out each taco shell and add a spoonful of the chicken filling. Top the chicken with shredded cheese and any other desired toppings such as salsa, sour cream, lettuce, tomatoes, olives, jalapeños, or guacamole.

Step-5

Serve the baked chicken tacos immediately and enjoy your delicious homemade meal!

Ingredients

1 pound (450g) boneless, skinless chicken breasts

2 tablespoons olive oil

1 small onion, finely chopped

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 packet (about 1.25 oz or 35g) taco seasoning mix (or use homemade seasoning)

1/2 cup chicken broth

Salt and pepper to taste

12 small taco shells or tortillas

2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract

1 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (or your favorite cheese)

Your favorite taco toppings (e.g., salsa, sour cream, shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, sliced olives, sliced jalapeños, guacamole)
