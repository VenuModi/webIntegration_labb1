Mediterranean Pasta Salad

This refreshing salad combines cooked orzo pasta with a medley of fresh ingredients like tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese. Perfect as a side dish or a light meal, this salad offers a taste of sunny Mediterranean shores, making it a favorite for summer gatherings and picnics.

Prep time

15 min.

Cook time

10 min.

Serves

serves 2

Instructions

Step-1

In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the orzo pasta according to the package instructions until it's al dente (usually about 8-10 minutes). Drain the cooked pasta and rinse it under cold water to stop the cooking process. Allow it to cool completely.

Step-2

In a small bowl, whisk together the extra-virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, Dijon mustard, honey (if using), salt, and black pepper. Adjust the seasoning to your taste. This is your flavorful dressing.

Step-3

In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooled orzo pasta, halved cherry tomatoes, diced cucumber, chopped red onion, sliced Kalamata olives, and crumbled feta cheese.Pour the dressing over the salad ingredients in the bowl.Add the chopped fresh basil and parsley leaves to the bowl as well. Gently toss all the ingredients together until they are well coated with the dressing and evenly mixed.

Step-4

Cover the bowl with plastic wrap or a lid and refrigerate the Mediterranean Orzo Pasta Salad for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld and develop.Before serving, give the salad a final toss and garnish with additional fresh herbs or feta cheese if desired.Serve the salad chilled as a side dish or a light and refreshing main course.

Ingredients

1 cup orzo pasta

1 cup cherry tomatoes-halved, 1 cucumber, diced, 1/2 red onion, finely chopped

1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced

1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese

1/4 cup fresh basil leaves, chopped

1/4 cup fresh parsley leaves, chopped

1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil

2 tablespoons red wine vinegar

1 clove garlic, minced

1 teaspoon Dijon mustard

1 teaspoon honey (optional, for sweetness)

Salt and black pepper to taste
