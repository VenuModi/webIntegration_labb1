Chilli Mac & Cheese

This hearty dish combines tender pasta with a rich, cheesy sauce and the spicy, savory flavors of chili. Whether you're craving a comforting weeknight dinner or looking to indulge in a satisfying meal on a chilly day, Chili Mac & Cheese offers the best of both worlds. With its creamy, cheesy goodness and a kick of chili heat, it's a flavorful and comforting fusion that's sure to satisfy your taste buds and warm your soul.

Prep time

15 min.

Cook time

35 min.

Serves

serves 5

Instructions

Step-1

In a large skillet or a deep saucepan, cook the ground beef over medium-high heat until it's browned and cooked through, breaking it apart with a spatula as it cooks. Add chopped onions and minced garlic to the cooked beef. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the onions become translucent. Stir in the kidney beans, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, cumin powder, salt, and black pepper. Mix well. Simmer the chili mixture over low heat for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld and the chili to thicken. Stir occasionally.

Step-2

While the chili is simmering, cook the elbow macaroni (or your chosen pasta) according to the package instructions until it's al dente. Drain and set aside.

Step-3

In a separate saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk continuously for about 1-2 minutes until it forms a smooth paste (a roux). Gradually add the milk while whisking constantly to prevent lumps. Continue to cook and whisk until the mixture thickens, about 5-7 minutes. Stir in the shredded cheese until it's fully melted and the sauce becomes smooth and creamy. Season with paprika, salt, and black pepper to taste.

Step-4

Combine the cooked and drained pasta with the chili mixture. Mix well until they are evenly combined. Pour the creamy cheese sauce over the pasta and chili mixture. Stir until everything is coated with the cheesy goodness.

Step-5

Serve your delicious Chili Mac & Cheese hot, garnished with additional shredded cheese, chopped green onions, or a dollop of sour cream, if desired.

Ingredients

1 pound (450g) ground beef (you can also use ground turkey or chicken)

1 medium onion, chopped, 2 cloves garlic, minced

1 can (15 ounces) kidney beans, drained and rinsed

1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes, 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce

2 tablespoons chili powder (adjust to taste for spiciness), 1 teaspoon cumin powder

Salt and black pepper to taste

2 cups (about 8 ounces) elbow macaroni or any pasta of your choice

2 cups shredded cheddar cheese (or a mix of cheddar and Monterey Jack)

2 tablespoons butter

2 tablespoons all-purpose flour

2 cups milk

1/2 teaspoon paprika
